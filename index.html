Projects

The Lyceum

My solo project for Emerging Digital Academy was the Lyceum Reading Group Manager. In the linked video, I am first to demonstrate starting around the six minute mark. It is a full stack React-Redux application designed to accomodate a global philosophy bookclub I belong to. It allows the creation of timezone based teams for students to coordinate meetings, share their reflections, and archive their intellectual labors. Completing reading groups awards members a badge that corresponds to that particular book as seen in the picture above. The project took me two weeks and was an extremely satisfying way to apply all that I learned! See the github here.

StartingLine.FM

My group client project for Emerging Digital Academy was the StartingLine.FM project. In the linked video, my group demonstrates first. It is another full stack React-Redux application built for the NDSU department of business and entrepreneurship. It is a database of resources within the Fargo, Moorhead, West Fargo area for budding entrepreneurs and business owners. It allows users to search for resources by business stage or other core categories. It then allows adding those resources to a to-do list which can be edited and exported. There is a three in one calendar that scrapes different institutions web calendar and puts in one place. It has full integration with a users google calendar too. This is what I primarily worked on during the project. Finally there is an admin portal where NDSU can edit, delete, and add resources, categories, and business stages. This was an incredible experience and way to cap off my EDA experience! See the github here and the website here.
